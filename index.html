Welcome to The Girly Pop Student Finance Tracker

This is a web application designed to help students (especially the girly pops) manage their finances effectively. You do not have to limit yourself but that does not mean you must spend willy nilly. Life is for living but living wisely. With our user-friendly interface, you can easily track your income and expenses, set budgets, and gain insights into your financial habits. Be as honest with yourself and your finances. Have fun grily pop. Get saving and spending!
